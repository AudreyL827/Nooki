✦ Nooki — Privacy Policy

Last updated: 11 July 2026

Nooki is a cozy pomodoro study game. We collect as little as possible and never sell your data. This page explains what is stored, why, and your choices.

What we store

  • Game progress — which furniture you’ve built, the short “win” notes you write, your chosen companion and timer preferences. Stored in your browser (localStorage) and, only if you sign in, in your private cloud save.
  • Account (optional) — if you choose “continue with Google,” we receive a Google account identifier so we can key your save to you. Your name/photo are shown only to you in the app. We never see your Google password. We do not store your email in the game database.
  • Spotify (optional) — if you connect Spotify, an access token is kept in your browser to control playback. We do not copy your Spotify library or listening history to any server of ours.

What we do NOT do

  • No selling or renting of personal data. No advertising networks.
  • We use Google Analytics only for basic, aggregate usage stats (visit counts, device type, broad geographic region) so we can understand overall traffic. It is never used for advertising or profiling, and nothing is sold. You can block it with any ad/tracker blocker.
  • No collection of data from children under 13. Nooki is not directed at children.

Where it lives & who processes it

Cloud saves are stored in Google Firebase (Firestore). Sign-in is handled by Google. Music playback uses Spotify. Each of these has its own privacy policy that applies when you use them. Your cloud save is isolated to your account by server-side security rules, so no other user can read it.

Security

All traffic is served over HTTPS. Cloud data is protected by per-user access rules (only you can read/write your saves/{your-id} document). Tokens live in your own browser. No system is perfectly secure, but we follow least-privilege and data-minimisation practices.

Your choices

  • Play without an account — everything stays local to your browser.
  • Sign out any time; disconnect Spotify any time (“unlink”).
  • Delete local data by clearing your browser storage for this site.
  • Request deletion of your cloud save by contacting us (below).
